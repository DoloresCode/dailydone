DailyDone

DailyDone is a streamlined project management tool crafted for efficiency and superb user-experience. Enabling users to easily create, track, update, and complete tasks and projects, it injects simplicity into every step of project management. The platform fosters productivity and team collaboration by providing features for task assignments, timeline creation, and real-time notifications. Whether managing personal tasks or coordinating team-based projects, DailyDone offers a comprehensive solution to visualize and monitor progress seamlessly. With its user-friendly interface and a wealth of features, it's an optimal choice for anyone seeking a superior solution for project management and task tracking. With DailyDone, we aim to turn every day into a day of accomplishment.

Key Features:

User Authentication

Register, Login, and Logout

Project Management

Create, View, Edit, and Delete projects

Task Management

Add, View, Update, and Delete tasks within projects

Collaboration

Assign tasks to specific collaborators and invite users to join projects

User Profile

With our user profiles, each individual can tailor their experience to fit their needs, enhancing productivity and providing a personalized touch to project management
